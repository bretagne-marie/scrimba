Visit Hägersten

Enjoy nature and culture in this cozy suburb close to the center of Stockholm.

Top 3 Activities To Do In Hägersten

Eat homegrown veggies at Vinterviken Café

Vinterviken Café is the perfect place enjoy a fresh, delicious lunch.

See a film or live jazz music at Tellus Biocafé

See an indie film or enjoy live music at this cozy, 100-year-old classic movie salon.

Check out modern art at Färgfabriken gallery

View innovative, culturally-abound modern art at this former paint factory.
